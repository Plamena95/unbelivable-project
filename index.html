WE ARE IDEOGEN

Pharma at the Speed of Life

IDEOGEN Group is a family owned Swiss pharmaceutical group, providing global access to specialty medicines for unmet medical needs.

We specialize in the commercialization of specialty medicines in Europe with challenging and evolving healthcare dynamics. With the operational infrastructure and know-how to handle activities from un-licensed through licensed medicines; we focus on contributing to unmet needs by offering healthcare providers access to unique treatment alternatives for medical challenges patients face today.

Since 2013 we have modelled our groups activities around underserved treatment options. Solving challenging problems that have an impact on patients and prescribers’ matter to us.

We approach our partnerships with transparency, loyalty and a sense of resolve. We believe in nurturing the awareness around innovative therapies, as they meet real needs of society today.

SOCIAL

OBLIGATION

Every aspect of our daily routine incorporates one main principle:

We want to add value to society by taking decisions that in one shape or another have immediate impact on the overall well-being of humanity.

Soliving real problems today while working on chronic solutions that fundamentally resolve regulatory, clinical and accessibility challenges of patients and healthcare professionals in different geographies is our goal.

Our commitment to society is that all of our processes have to be green, socially attuned and strive to establish a win-win sustainable and concrete value proposition that is aligned with the needs of a global community.
